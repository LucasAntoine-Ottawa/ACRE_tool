Important limitations: These estimates are based on population-level data and should not replace personalized medical advice. Risk estimates use 10-year cumulative incidence and are adjusted for competing mortality (Statistics Canada) and alcohol prevalence (CCHS). Head & neck cancer risk uses relative risk data for oral cavity/pharynx cancers applied to all head & neck cancers — this may overestimate laryngeal cancer risk.
